    Tennessee Knoxville Mission

    L. Mark Barlow and Kimberly A. Douros Barlow, three children, Westland 9th Ward, West Jordan Utah Westland Stake: Tennessee Knoxville Mission. President Barlow is a former stake president, stake presidency counselor, bishop, bishopric counselor, elders quorum president, ward Young Men president, and missionary in the Hawaii Honolulu Mission. He was born in Murray, Utah, to Lincoln Farrell Barlow and Marilyn Sanders Barlow. Sister Barlow is a former patriarch scribe, ward Young Women president, ward Relief Society presidency counselor, ward Primary secretary, ward Young Women adviser, Primary chorister, Sunday School teacher, and ward temple and family history consultant. She was born in Salt Lake City, Utah, to John David Douros and Gayle Anne Hufham Douros.
